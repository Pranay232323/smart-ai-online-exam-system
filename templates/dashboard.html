Exam Rules And Regulations

Please read these instructions carefully before starting the exam.

  • Keep a stable internet connection throughout the exam.
  • Do not switch tabs or minimize the exam window.
  • Stay in fullscreen mode if the exam enters it.
  • Copy, paste, cut, and right-click actions are monitored.
  • Reloading or refreshing during the exam triggers a warning.
  • After 3 warnings, the exam will be submitted automatically.
  • When the timer ends, the exam is submitted automatically.
