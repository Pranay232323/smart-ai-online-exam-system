Exam Rules And Regulations

Please read these instructions carefully before starting the exam.

  • Make sure you have a stable internet connection before starting.
  • Do not switch tabs or leave the exam window during the test.
  • Reloading or refreshing during the exam may trigger a warning.
  • When the timer ends, the exam will be submitted automatically.
  • Read each question carefully before choosing your answer.
  • Click submit only when you are ready to finish the exam.
